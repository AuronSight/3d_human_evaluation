Mesh Lighting Study

This desktop study measures how lighting affects the perceived readability and visual quality of 3D meshes.

Instructions

  • Use left-drag to orbit, right-drag to pan, and the wheel to zoom.
  • Inspect each mesh freely, then rate it on two seven-point scales.
  • Keep display brightness and room lighting unchanged during the session.
  • The study is local: responses remain in this browser until you export the CSV.

24 trials · 12 meshes × 2 lighting conditions per participant · balanced across four lighting designs. Demographic fields are optional and stored only in your exported CSV.
